Ingredients

You can tell us about the ingredients in a cosmetic product by:

  • choosing a ‘frame formulation’ that matches the product most closely
  • uploading a PDF with the ‘concentration ranges’ of the ingredients (for example, above 0.1% w/w up to 1% w/w)
  • uploading a PDF with the ‘exact concentrations’ of the ingredients (% w/w)
If the product was placed on the market after 31 January 2020, you'll also need to tell us the exact concentration of any ingredients the National Poison Information Service (NPIS) needs to know about in a separate document.
