Concentration ranges of the ingredients

To tell us about the concentration ranges of the ingredients, you'll need to upload a PDF which lists the ingredients.

The National Poison Information Service (NPIS) needs to know about some ingredients in the product – list these first with the exact concentrations.

Ingredients NPIS needs to know about
{% include "../includes/poisonous-ingredients.html" %}

What you'll need to tell us about the ingredients

For each ingredient in the product, you'll need to tell us the:

  • International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients (INCI) name
  • Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) number
  • concentration range (% w/w)

List the ingredients in order of the largest concentrations first.

Concentration ranges

Use these concentration ranges:

  • up to and including 0.1% w/w
  • above 0.1% w/w up to 1% w/w
  • above 1% w/w up to 5% w/w
  • above 5% w/w up to 10% w/w
  • above 10% w/w up to 25% w/w
  • above 25% w/w up to 50% w/w
  • above 50% w/w up to 75% w/w
  • above 75% w/w up to 100% w/w

Shades

For products available in shades, clearly list the difference between shades.

If any ingredient NPIS needs to know about differs between shades, you must submit them as seperate notitications.
