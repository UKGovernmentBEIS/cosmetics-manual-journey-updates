Multi-item kits

Multi-item kits are composed of items that are not marketed separately. They are sold as a kit and intended to be used as a mix or sequentially.

Examples of multi-component cosmetics include:

  • hair dye sets
  • skin peeling kits
  • permanent styling products
  • anti-age or revitalising kits

If the cosmetic product is sold as a gift set where the components are not mixed or used sequentially, submit a notification for each product separately.
