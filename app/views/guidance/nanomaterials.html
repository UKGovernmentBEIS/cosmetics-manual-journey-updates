Tell us about nanomaterials

You need to tell us about all the nanomaterials used in your cosmetic product.

How you do this depends on whether the substance is listed as a colourant, preservative or UV filter in EC Regulation 1223/2009 (Annex 4, 5 or 6).

Substances listed in EC Regulation 1223/2009

If the substance is listed in EC Regulation 1223/2009, you can tell us about the nanomaterial when you notify us about the cosmetic product.

You'll need to tell us the International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients (INCI) name of each substance.

Substances not listed in EC Regulation 1223/2009

If the substance is not listed in EC Regulation 1223/2009, or not used in a standard way, you'll need to give us more information about the nanomaterial before you can notify us about the cosmetic product.

You'll need to send a PDF to nanomaterial-review-panel@beis.gov.uk listing the nanomaterials.

For each nanomaterial, you'll need to tell us:

  • the quantity expected to be placed on the market each year
  • the specification, including the size of particles, physical and chemical properties
  • the droplet size and distribution when sprayed
  • the toxicological profile
  • the safety data relating to the category of products in which it will be used
  • whether it reacts or changes its properties in certain conditions, for example if it's photocatalytic
You'll also need to tell us the email address of someone to contact about the nanomaterials.
