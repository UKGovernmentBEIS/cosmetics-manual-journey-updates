Tell us about nanomaterials

If the nanomaterials in the cosmetic product are not listed as colourants, preservatives or UV filters under EC Regulation 1223/2009 (Annex 4, 5 or 6), you'll need to upload a PDF with the following information:

  • the quantity of nanomaterials expected to be placed on the market each year
  • the specification, including the size of particles, physical and chemical properties
  • the droplet size and distribution when sprayed
  • the toxicological profile
  • the safety data relating to the category of products in which they'll be used
  • whether they react or change their properties in certain conditions, for example if they're photocatalytic

You'll also need to tell us:

  • when the product was first notified if it was notified with the EU before 1 February 2020
  • the email address of someone to contact about the nanomaterials
