Tell us about nanomaterials

How you tell us about the nanomaterials in your cosmetic product depends on whether the substance in the nanomaterial is listed in Annex 4, 5 or 6 of EC regulation 1223/2009.

Substances listed in Annex 4, 5 or 6

If the substance in the nanomaterial is listed in Annex 4, 5 or 6 of EC regulation 1223/2009, you can tell us about these nanomaterials when you notify us about the cosmetic product.

Substances not listed in Annex 4, 5 or 6

If the substance in the nanomaterial is not listed in Annex 4, 5 or 6 of EC regulation 1223/2009, you'll need to tell us about these nanomaterials before you notify us about the cosmetic product.

Email a PDF file to: nanomaterial-review-panel@beis.gov.uk.
What you'll need to tell us about the nanomaterial

You'll need to tell us:

  • the quantity expected to be placed on the market each year
  • its specification, including the size of particles, physical and chemical properties
  • the droplet size and distribution when sprayed
  • its toxicological profile
  • its safety data relating to the category of products in which it will be used
  • whether it reacts or changes its properties in certain conditions, for example, if it is photocatalytic

You'll also need to tell us whether the product was notified in the EU before 1 November 2019 (Brexit) and if so, when it was first notified.
