Search for cosmetic products available in the UK and notified with the Office for Product Safety and Standards (OPSS).

How to access the service

National Poison Information Service (NPIS) staff

If you're using this service for the first time, you'll need to email opss.enquiries@beis.gov.uk to ask for an account.

Trading Standards officers

You can use your Product Safety Database (PSD) sign in details (username and password). If you don't have these, you'll need to ask the person in your organisation responsible for PSD.

What you can search for

You can search for cosmetic products by:

  • product name
  • product category

NPIS staff

You can find out about the ingredents in a cosmetics product.

Trading Standards officers

You can check if the cosmetic product has been notified with OPSS.

What you can't search for

You can't search for cosmetic products taken off the UK market before 1 November 2019 but still used in the UK.

Some ingredient information and packaging images may be missing for cosmetic products notified with the EU before 1 November 2019.
