All cosmetic products available to consumers in the UK must have Responsible Person and be notified in the UK.

Responsible Persons

Every cosmetic product placed on the UK market must have a Responsible Person (RP). The RP can be a business or a natural person. The RP must make sure:

  • cosmetic products placed on the market are safe for human health
  • each cosmetic product complies with Regulation 1223/2009

Product notifications

You need to submit a notification for each product availble to consumers. This includes products already notified on the EU Cosmetic Product Notification Portal (CPNP) before 31 October 2019 (Brexit) and products placed on the market since.

You do not have to submit notifications for cosmetic products taken off the market in the UK before 31 October 2019.

Find out how to submit a notification and what information you need to provide

Non-compliance

For products on the market before Brexit you must submit your notifications for these products by 11 pm GMT on 29 January 2020.

For new products you must submit a notification before placing the product on the market.

If you do not, you may face a penalty of up to £5,000 and a prison term of up to 3 months, or both.
